Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

NOVA Studio ("we", "us", or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy and handling your personal information responsibly. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, disclose, and protect your personal information in accordance with the Australian Privacy Act 1988 (Cth) and the Australian Privacy Principles (APPs).

1. Information We Collect

Account information:

  • Full name and email address provided at registration
  • Password (stored in hashed form — we cannot read it)
  • Profile photo (if you choose to upload one)
  • Referral code (if provided)

Usage and activity information:

  • Credits used and full transaction history
  • Subscription plan and billing history
  • Content you generate including lyrics, style prompts, and project names
  • Content you publish to the Explore community feed
  • Voice samples you upload for voice cloning (see Voice Data section below)
  • Likes, comments, and follows within the Explore community
  • App usage patterns including features used and session duration

Device and technical information:

  • Device type, manufacturer, and operating system version
  • App version number
  • Crash reports and error logs (anonymised where possible)
  • IP address (collected by our hosting provider Vercel for security purposes)

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To create and manage your account and authenticate your identity
  • To process subscription payments and track credit balances
  • To provide AI generation features by routing your prompts through our secure backend
  • To operate the Explore community feed and enable social features
  • To send transactional notifications (generation complete, subscription renewal, low credits)
  • To send marketing notifications only where you have explicitly opted in
  • To detect and prevent fraud, abuse, and violations of our Terms of Service
  • To improve the app, fix bugs, and develop new features using aggregated, anonymised data
  • To comply with legal obligations including responding to valid court orders and law enforcement requests
  • To respond to copyright takedown notices and content reports

3. Push Notifications — Spam Act 2003

Under Australia's Spam Act 2003, we distinguish between two types of push notifications:

  • Transactional notifications — generation complete, payment receipts, subscription renewals, account security alerts. These are necessary for the Service to function and do not require separate marketing consent.
  • Marketing notifications — weekly creator digest, new features, promotional offers. These require your explicit opt-in consent, which you can manage at any time in the Notifications settings within the app.

4. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes.

We share limited information with the following service providers solely to operate the Service:

  • Supabase (Singapore) — stores your account data, credit balance, and project history in an encrypted database. Supabase is SOC 2 Type II certified.
  • Stripe (United States) — processes all subscription payments and top-up purchases. Your full card details are handled exclusively by Stripe and are never transmitted to or stored by NOVA Studio. Stripe is PCI DSS Level 1 certified.
  • Anthropic (United States) — your text prompts (lyrics topics, style descriptions, chat messages) are sent to Claude AI for processing. Anthropic's privacy policy governs this data. Anthropic does not use API data to train its models by default.
  • AI generation providers — your style prompts are sent to third party generation services (Suno, Udio, Runway, Flux, Kling, and others) to generate music, images, and video. The specific provider used depends on which engine you select.
  • Vercel (United States) — hosts our backend API. Vercel may log request metadata including IP addresses for security monitoring.
  • Expo (United States) — used to build and distribute the mobile application.

We may also disclose your personal information where required by Australian law, a court order, or to protect the safety of our users or the public.

All third party providers are bound by data processing agreements and are required to handle your information only as directed by us and in accordance with applicable privacy law.

5. Voice Data

Voice samples you upload for the voice cloning feature are stored securely on our servers and used exclusively to create a personal voice model linked to your account. We do not share your voice data with third parties and do not use it to train AI models.

You can delete your saved voice models at any time through the Voices tab in the app. Upon deletion your voice data is permanently removed from our systems within 30 days.

We take particular care with voice data given its biometric nature. Access to voice data within our systems is strictly limited and logged.

6. Cross-Border Data Transfers

Your data is processed and stored by service providers in the United States and Singapore. We ensure that such transfers are conducted under appropriate safeguards. Where data is transferred outside Australia, we take steps to ensure the recipient handles it in a way that is consistent with the Australian Privacy Principles.

7. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including:

  • HTTPS encryption for all data in transit
  • AES-256 encryption for sensitive data at rest
  • Bcrypt hashing for all passwords
  • Row-level security policies on our database
  • Regular security audits and access controls

Despite these measures, no system is completely secure. We recommend using a strong, unique password and enabling two-factor authentication where available.

8. Data Breach Notification

In accordance with the Australian Privacy Act's Notifiable Data Breaches scheme, if we become aware of an eligible data breach that is likely to result in serious harm to any affected individuals, we will:

  • Notify the Office of the Australian Information Commissioner (OAIC) as soon as practicable
  • Notify affected individuals directly by email
  • Publish a statement on our website if direct notification is not reasonably practicable

9. Data Retention

We retain your account information for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, we will delete your personal information within 30 days, with the following exceptions:

  • Financial transaction records are retained for 7 years as required by Australian tax law
  • Content you published to Explore may remain visible to other users unless you delete it before account closure
  • Aggregated, anonymised usage data may be retained indefinitely for service improvement

10. Your Rights Under Australian Privacy Law

Under the Australian Privacy Act 1988 you have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate or out-of-date information
  • Request deletion of your personal information (subject to legal retention requirements)
  • Complain about a breach of the Australian Privacy Principles
  • Request information about how we handle personal information

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at privacy@novastudio.app. We will respond within 30 days.

If you are not satisfied with our response, you may lodge a complaint with the Office of the Australian Information Commissioner (OAIC) at oaic.gov.au.

11. Your Rights Under GDPR (European Users)

If you are located in the European Economic Area, you have additional rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) including the right to data portability and the right to object to processing. Our legal basis for processing your data is performance of contract (providing the Service) and legitimate interests (security and fraud prevention). Contact us at privacy@novastudio.app to exercise your GDPR rights.

12. California Residents — CCPA

NOVA Studio does not sell personal information. We do not sell, share for cross-context behavioural advertising, or otherwise monetise your personal data to third parties.

California residents have the right to know what personal information is collected, the right to deletion, and the right to non-discrimination. To exercise these rights contact privacy@novastudio.app.

13. Children's Privacy

NOVA Studio is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that a child under 13 has provided personal information without verifiable parental consent, we will delete that information immediately.

If you believe a child under 13 has created an account, please contact us immediately at support@novastudio.app.

14. Cookies and Tracking

The NOVA Studio mobile app does not use cookies. Our backend servers may use standard access logs to monitor performance, diagnose issues, and detect abuse. We do not use third party advertising trackers, behavioural profiling tools, or sell data to advertisers. NOVA Studio is an ad-free platform.

15. Third Party Links and Social Platforms

The app may contain links to third party services including social media platforms (TikTok, Instagram, YouTube, Twitter/X, Facebook). When you share content to these platforms, your use of those platforms is governed by their own privacy policies. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these services.

16. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or applicable law. We will notify you of material changes by email or in-app notification at least 14 days before the change takes effect. The date at the top of this policy indicates when it was last updated.
