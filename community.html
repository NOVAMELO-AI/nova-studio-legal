Community Guidelines

Last updated: July 2026

NOVA Studio is a creative community. These guidelines exist to keep it a positive, inspiring, and safe space for everyone. By publishing content to the Explore feed, you agree to follow these guidelines. Violations may result in content removal, account suspension, or permanent termination.

Our Core Values

Be creative. Be kind. Be original. Respect other creators and respect the law.

What We Encourage ✓

  • Original creative work — music, images, videos, and music videos you genuinely made with NOVA Studio
  • Supportive comments and constructive feedback on other creators' work
  • Sharing your creative process and what prompts produced great results
  • Experimenting with genres, styles, and visual concepts
  • Collaborating with other NOVA Studio users
  • Celebrating other creators with likes and follows

Content Rules

Intellectual Property

  • Do not publish content designed to infringe or reproduce copyrighted material
  • Do not use style prompts specifically designed to exactly replicate a copyrighted song or artwork
  • Do not publish AI-generated content that closely imitates the distinctive expression of a specific copyrighted work

Using style references for inspiration is fine. Attempting to clone or reproduce specific copyrighted works is not.

Voice and Likeness

  • Do not publish content that uses a cloned voice of any real person without their explicit written consent
  • Do not publish content designed to make listeners believe it was actually recorded by a real artist or public figure
  • Do not impersonate other NOVA Studio users or real people in your profile or content

AI Content Disclosure

All content published on NOVA Studio is automatically labelled as "AI Generated." Do not attempt to remove or obscure this label, or publish content elsewhere claiming it was created without AI when it was generated using NOVA Studio.

Harmful Content

  • No sexual content of any kind — including suggestive content involving minors
  • No graphic violence, gore, or content that glorifies harm to people or animals
  • No content that promotes, glorifies, or facilitates terrorism, extremism, or mass violence
  • No content that promotes hatred or discrimination based on race, ethnicity, religion, gender, sexual orientation, disability, age, or nationality
  • No content designed to harass, threaten, or intimidate specific individuals
  • No content that facilitates illegal activity including drug trafficking, weapons sales, or fraud

Spam and Manipulation

  • No repetitive or near-identical posts flooding the Explore feed
  • No coordinated inauthentic behaviour to artificially inflate likes or follows
  • No use of bots or automated tools to interact with the community
  • No misleading thumbnails, titles, or descriptions

Comments

Keep comments constructive and respectful. You may disagree with someone's creative choices but must do so politely. Comments that are abusive, harassing, threatening, or contain slurs will be removed and may result in account suspension.

Reporting Content

If you see content that violates these guidelines, tap the Report button on the post and select the reason. We review all reports within 24 hours. For copyright infringement reports, email copyright@novastudio.app with details of the infringing content.

False or bad faith reports are themselves a violation of these guidelines.

Enforcement

We enforce these guidelines using a combination of automated detection and human review. Depending on the severity and frequency of violations:

  • Content may be removed without notice
  • Accounts may receive a warning
  • Accounts may be temporarily suspended
  • Accounts may be permanently terminated without refund

We reserve the right to remove any content or terminate any account that we determine, in our sole discretion, violates these guidelines or harms the community.

Appeals

If you believe your content was removed in error, contact us at support@novastudio.app with a description of the content and why you believe it complies with our guidelines. We will review your appeal within 5 business days.
